Overview

Steven "Hans Sama" Liv is a League of Legends esports player, currently bot laner for G2 Esports.

  2. Reached 1000 kills in the LEC on August 13th, 2021.
  3. The 8th player to reach 1000 kills in the LEC after Rekkles, Jankos, Perkz, nukeduck, Caps, Wunder and Kobbe.
