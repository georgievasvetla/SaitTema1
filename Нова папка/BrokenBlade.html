BrokenBlade

Overview

Sergen "BrokenBlade" Çelik is a League of Legends esports player, currently top laner for G2 Esports. His name was previously stylized Broken Blade.

Personal Information

  • Name - Sergen Çelik
  • Country of Birth - Germany
  • Nationality - Germany, Turkey
  • Birthday - January 19, 2000 (age 23)
  • Residency - EMEA (Europe)

Trivia

  1. Has been a fan of Turkish football club Galatasaray since his childhood.
  2. Is a dual citizen of Germany and Turkey.
  3. First Turkish resident to play in the LCS.
  4. Was the subject of good-natured memes by his teammates after joining TSM regarding his short stature.
  5. His ID is a reference to the weapon of the champion Riven.
  6. Is the 3rd player to win a title in both LEC and LCS after Zven and Perkz.
