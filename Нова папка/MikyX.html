MikyX

Overview

Mihael "Mikyx" Mehle is a League of Legends esports player, currently support for G2 Esports.

Personal Information

  • Name - Mihael Mehle
  • Country of Birth - Slovenia
  • Nationality - Slovenia
  • Birthday - November 2, 1998 (age 24)
  • Residency - EMEA (Europe)

Trivia

  1. Favorite football player is Michael Ballack.
  2. Favorite TV show is Game of Thrones.
  3. Favorite animes are Code Geass: Lelouch of the Rebellion and Hunter x Hunter.
  4. Sometimes makes appearances on LS' streams.
  5. Reached 2500 Assists in the LEC on August 22, 2020.
  6. The 4th player to reach 2500 assists in the LEC after Hylissang, Jankos and VandeR.
  7. Reached 1000 deaths in the LEC on July 30, 2021.
  8. His 1000th death was done by MAD Lions's Carzzy in the LEC 2021 Summer Split with Varus.
