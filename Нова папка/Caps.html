Caps

Overview

Rasmus "Caps" Borregaard Winther is a League of Legends esports player, currently mid laner for G2 Esports. His name was briefly stylized caPs.

Personal Information

  • Name - Rasmus Borregaard Winther
  • Country of Birth - Denmark
  • Nationality - Denmark
  • Birthday - November 17, 1999 (age 23)
  • Residency - EMEA (Europe)

Trivia

  1. Is the younger brother of DotA 2 player Ryze.
  2. Nicknamed Baby Faker by fans.
  3. His father frequently attends his games, including traveling to Vietnam and Taiwan to see his son win the 2019 Mid-Season Invitational.
  4. He and Uzi are the only two non-Korean players to have appeared in consecutive Worlds finals.
  5. As of July 2021, Became famous for his shaved head.
  6. Frequently says Champion quotes out loud when picking or banning a Champion.
  7. Reached 1000 kills in the LEC on June 12, 2020.
  8. Reached 1500 kills in the LEC on April 3, 2022.
